About Me


I’m currently a Program Manager for a technology company with years of experience in the software development processes, including developing SRS documentation, software QA, acceptance testing and change management. I currently manage several contracts and offices in the western region of the United States.

Originally from Chicago, Illinois I’ve lived in Salt Lake City Utah for the past several years. The stunning landscapes of the American West have been a great inspiration for another passion of mine, photography. With interests in landscape, wildlife and fashion editorials I’ve run an active photography studio since 2014.
